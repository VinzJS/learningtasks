Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Can coconut milk, or low fat coconut milk
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Chopped green chili
  • Thai green curry paste

Method

  • 1)Cook the paste in a arge nonstick wok or large frying pan over a low heat, for 5 minutes.
  • 2)Cut the chicken into strips and add to the pan with the chopped onion.
  • 3)Cook for 5 to 8 minutes or until no longer pink and add the aubergine chopped.
  • 4)Stir in the coconut milk and simmer for about 10 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  • 5)Scatter over the chili and serve with rice.
