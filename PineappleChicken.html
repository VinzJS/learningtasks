4 Servings

Pineapple Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 Tsp canola or sesame oil
  • 1 (10oz) package frozen broccoli (or stir fry vegetable mix),thawed
  • 1/2 C stir fry sauce
  • 1/4 C pineapple juice, use reserved juice from canned pineapple
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (optional: use if you like it hot!)
  • 1 (15 oz) can pineapple chunks or tidbits, drained (reserve the juice!)
  • 2 C diced cooked chicken or 2 (10 oz) cans chicken breast, drained and flaked

Method

  • 1)Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  • 2)Add all ingredients EXCEPT the pineapple & chicken.
  • 3)Cook and stir until heated through, 5 to 6 min.
  • 4)Add pineapple and chicken; cook another 2 minutes.
  • 5)Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta.
  • 6.) Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold running water for several minutes
